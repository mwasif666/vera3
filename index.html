Relax in our mineral-rich thermal baths that help detoxify the body, improve circulation, and relieve muscle tension. Our thermal baths are maintained at optimal temperatures between 36-40°C for maximum therapeutic benefits. Each session includes aromatherapy options and post-bath relaxation in our heated loungers.
Our specialized sports massage therapy focuses on athletes and active individuals. Using deep tissue techniques combined with stretching, we help prevent injuries, improve flexibility, and enhance recovery time. Choose from 30, 60, or 90-minute sessions with our certified sports massage therapists.
Our customized facial treatments begin with a skin analysis to determine your specific needs. Options include hydrating, anti-aging, brightening, and acne-control facials. Each treatment includes deep cleansing, exfoliation, extractions (if needed), facial massage, mask, and hydration with premium skincare products.
Experience virtually pain-free hair removal with our professional waxing services. We use high-quality hard and soft waxes suitable for all skin types. Services include eyebrows, face, underarms, arms, legs, bikini, and Brazilian waxing. Our estheticians apply pre- and post-wax treatments to soothe skin and prevent irritation.
Our manicure services begin with a warm soak and exfoliation, followed by cuticle care, nail shaping, and a hydrating hand and arm massage. Choose from classic polish, long-lasting gel, or our signature spa manicure that includes a paraffin wax treatment. All services finish with cuticle oil and hand cream application.
Treat your feet to our luxurious pedicures that include a warm foot soak, exfoliation with natural scrubs, callus removal, nail shaping, cuticle care, and a relaxing foot and lower leg massage. Upgrade to our spa pedicure for additional treatments like a hydrating mask and paraffin wax dip.
Our expert stylists provide precision haircuts tailored to your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Services include consultation, shampoo with scalp massage, professional cut using advanced techniques, blow-dry and style. We specialize in all hair types from straight to curly, fine to thick.
Our professional beard grooming service includes washing with beard shampoo, precise trimming and shaping with scissors and clippers, detailed edging, hot towel treatment, and application of beard oil or balm. We'll help you maintain your preferred style while keeping your beard healthy and well-groomed.
Our professional makeup artists create customized looks using high-quality, hypoallergenic products. Services range from natural daytime makeup to full glam for special occasions. Includes consultation, skin prep, color matching, application, and finishing touches. Perfect for weddings, photoshoots, or just treating yourself.
